Project 3

Saline Level Monitoring System

IR Transmitter & Receiver pair are opposite to each other & Transmitter Is given a supply constantly so it glows constantly during operation of device. As drop interrupts the light beam the receive voltage drop and generates an interrupt to node MCU.Drops are counted and from these information rate of drops,volume remain inside the bottle,time remain for bottle to get empty and calculated.Data is send to firebase database via internet. From database , data is received by Android App. Notification is generate with sound and vibration on smartphone when amount of liquid remain 25% ,20%,10% of total amount.
